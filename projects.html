Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Indore(Sep 2017 - Aug 2018)

Project: Traffic Dynamics-Aware Probe Selection for Fault Detection in Networks

Mentors: Dr. Neminath Hubballi and Dr Maitreya Natu

Computer networks are growing exponentially and require guarantee up-time. Any unchecked failure/congestion can bring whole network down, costing thousands of dollars to service provider.
  • We formulated a greedy algorithm for probe selection (failure identifying mechanism) which optimizes timeliness, cost and localizes fault instantly in networks.
  • Considered the problem of selecting probes that adapt to changes in traffic dynamics experienced by nodes to identify faults in computer networks.
  • Showed that probe selection optimization problem is {NP-Complete}
  • Formulated a linear programming optimization problem to select a set of probes to pass through minimally congested nodes.
  • Proposed two greedy algorithms which work by restricting the length of probes to K.
